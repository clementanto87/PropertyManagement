# Prioritized List of 100 Best External Integrations for a German Property Management App

This list is prioritized based on the following criteria, reflecting the unique demands of the German property management market:
1.  **Criticality & Compliance (P1):** Integrations essential for legal operation and core financial processes (e.g., German accounting, utility billing, major portals).
2.  **Market Dominance & Efficiency (P2):** Integrations with market-leading German PropTechs and tools that significantly boost manager efficiency and tenant satisfaction.
3.  **Strategic & Future-Proofing (P3):** Integrations that offer a competitive edge, such as advanced IoT, data analytics, and embedded finance.
